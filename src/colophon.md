<h1 class="page-title">colophon</h1>

<div class="prose">

A few things I think you should know about this site.

On the writing: the ideas here are mine. I think about them, sit with them, and try to write them down in my own words. But I'm not going to pretend I don't use AI. I do — mostly to polish rough drafts, tighten sentences, and catch things I miss. The thinking is mine. The first draft is usually mine. The final version is a collaboration.

I'm aiming to get to a place where I don't need that step. Writing clearly is a skill, and I'm still building it. Using AI as a crutch while learning to walk on my own feels more honest than either pretending I don't use it or letting it do all the work.

If something on this site resonates with you, the thought behind it came from a real person. That's what I can promise.
